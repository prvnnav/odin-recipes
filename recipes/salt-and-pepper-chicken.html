Salt and pepper chicken

Crispy fried chicken served with spicy sliced onions and chilli

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast, sliced into strips
  • Cornstarch
  • White onion
  • Red chilli
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chilli flakes for an extra kick

Steps

  1. Dredge your chicken strips in a cornstarch coating.
  2. Shallow fry the coated strips until golden brown.
  3. Add thinly sliced onions and chilli.
  4. Add seasonings.
  5. Mix and stir until onions are fragrant.

Egg Fried Rice

Perfect fluffy egg fried rice topped with spring onion garnish

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • 2 medium eggs
  • Spring onions
  • Salt and pepper to season

Steps

  1. Crack your eggs into a bowl and whisk until smooth.
  2. Add a neutral oil to a wok and heat.
  3. Add beaten eggs and agitate.
  4. Stir in the cooked rice.
  5. Once egg pieces begin to set, take off heat and plate.
  6. Garnish with thinly sliced spring onions.

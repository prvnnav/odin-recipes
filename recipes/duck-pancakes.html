Duck pancakes

Duck meat wrapped in a pancake with cucumber, spring onions and hoisin sauce

Ingredients

  • Duck meat
  • Cucumber, thinly sliced
  • Spring onions, thinly sliced
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Thin pancakes

Steps

  1. Cook duck meat until tender.
  2. Pull apart with forks to make strands for pancakes.
  3. Spread hoisin sauce liberally on pancakes.
  4. Add thinly sliced cucumber and spring onion as desired.
  5. Add duck meat to pancake.
  6. Roll securely and serve.
